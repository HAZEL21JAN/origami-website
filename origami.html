Interesting facts about chameleon

  • These are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of of communication among chameleons
  • most of these reptiles have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about pigeon

  • These are incredibly intelligent and complex animals
  • pigeons are renowed for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • they even have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting facts about panda

  • At 5 months old, giant pandas learn how to climb - sometimes practicing by climbing on their mum.
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.
  • Like domestic cats, giant pandas have vertical slits for pupils.

Interesting facts about cisada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.
